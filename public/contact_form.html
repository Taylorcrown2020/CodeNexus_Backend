// Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take? +
Starter packages typically take 2-3 weeks. Professional packages take 4-6 weeks. Enterprise solutions vary based on complexity, but we'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
Do you offer payment plans? +
Yes! We typically split payments into milestones: 50% upfront, 25% at mid-project review, and 25% upon completion. For enterprise projects, we can create custom payment schedules.
What happens after my website launches? +
All packages include post-launch support. We'll monitor your site, fix any bugs, and provide training on how to manage content. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages if you need continued support.
Can you redesign my existing website? +
Absolutely! We can redesign and rebuild your existing site with modern technologies. We'll migrate all your content and ensure everything works perfectly before going live.
Do you provide hosting and domain services? +
Yes! All our projects are deployed on Render with PostgreSQL databases included. We can also help you purchase and configure your domain name if needed.
What if I need changes after the project is complete? +
Your support period covers bug fixes and minor tweaks. For major changes or new features, we offer hourly rates or can create a new project proposal based on your needs.
