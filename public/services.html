Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services. Can't find the answer you're looking for? Just reach out.

What's included in the base price? +
All packages include design, development, deployment, and the specified support period. You'll get a fully functional website with no hidden costs. The only additional expenses would be for custom features beyond the package scope, any third-party platforms, integrations, usage-based fees or ongoing hosting fees (typically $20-50/month).
How long does it take to build a website? +
Starter packages typically take 4 weeks, Professional packages take 6 weeks, and Enterprise projects vary based on complexity (usually 8-16 weeks). Timelines depend on how quickly you provide feedback and content during the project.
Do you offer payment plans? +
Full payment is required upon completion of the project. All intellectual property rights, source code, and deliverables remain the property of Diamondback Coding until payment is received in full. Enterprise projects may qualify for milestone-based payment structures subject to written agreement.
What happens after the support period ends? +
Upon completion of the included support period, extended maintenance services are available starting at $59.99 per month. This service includes bug fixes, security updates, and minor content modifications. Hosting services and any third-party platforms are not included and will be billed separately.
Can I upgrade my package later? +
Absolutely! Many clients start with our Starter package and upgrade as they grow. We'll credit your initial payment toward the upgraded package. We're here to support your business at every stage of growth.
Will I own my website code? +
Yes! Upon final payment, you receive full ownership of all code, designs, and assets. We'll provide you with complete source code and documentation. You're free to host it anywhere and make changes as needed.
What's included in your SEO services? +
Our $199/month SEO service includes keyword research, on-page optimization, technical SEO, Google My Business management, local citation building, content optimization, and monthly performance reports. We focus on getting Hill Country businesses ranked for keywords that actually drive customers.
How much does digital marketing cost? +
Pricing is tailored to each campaign based on complexity, platforms used, and strategic goals. We work with you to design a marketing plan that aligns with your objectives and budget, and provide a custom quote before any work begins.
Can I bundle website development with SEO or marketing? +
Absolutely! We offer package deals when you combine website development with ongoing SEO or digital marketing services. This ensures your new site is optimized from day one and starts attracting customers immediately. Contact us for bundled pricing.
