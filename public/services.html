Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and services. Can't find the answer you're looking for? Just reach out.

What's included in the base price? +
All packages include design, development, deployment, and the specified support period. You'll get a fully functional website with no hidden costs. The only additional expenses would be for custom features beyond the package scope or ongoing hosting fees (typically $20-50/month).
How long does it take to build a website? +
Starter packages typically take 4 weeks, Professional packages take 6 weeks, and Enterprise projects vary based on complexity (usually 8-16 weeks). Timelines depend on how quickly you provide feedback and content during the project.
Do you offer payment plans? +
Yes! We offer flexible payment plans for all packages. Typically, we split payments into 50% upfront and 50% upon completion. For Enterprise projects, we can structure milestone-based payments. Contact us to discuss payment options that work for your budget.
What happens after the support period ends? +
After your included support period, you can choose to purchase extended support packages starting at $39.99/month. This covers bug fixes, security updates, and minor content changes. Major updates or new features would be quoted separately.
Can I upgrade my package later? +
Absolutely! Many clients start with our Starter package and upgrade as they grow. We'll credit your initial payment toward the upgraded package. We're here to support your business at every stage of growth.
Will I own my website code? +
Yes! Upon final payment, you receive full ownership of all code, designs, and assets. We'll provide you with complete source code and documentation. You're free to host it anywhere and make changes as needed.
Do you provide hosting? +
We deploy your site on Render.com, which offers excellent performance and reliability. Hosting costs are separate and typically range from $20-50/month depending on traffic. We'll help you set up hosting, and you'll pay Render directly.
What's included in your SEO services? +
Our $299/month SEO service includes keyword research, on-page optimization, technical SEO, Google My Business management, local citation building, content optimization, and monthly performance reports. We focus on getting Hill Country businesses ranked for keywords that actually drive customers.
How much does digital marketing cost? +
Digital marketing pricing varies based on campaign complexity, ad spend budget, and platforms. Most Hill Country businesses invest $1,000-$5,000/month including ad spend. We provide custom quotes based on your specific goals and budget.
Can I bundle website development with SEO or marketing? +
Absolutely! We offer package deals when you combine website development with ongoing SEO or digital marketing services. This ensures your new site is optimized from day one and starts attracting customers immediately. Contact us for bundled pricing.
What if I need features not listed in the packages? +
No problem! We can customize any package or build completely custom solutions. Just tell us what you need, and we'll provide a detailed quote. Enterprise clients often need unique features, and we're equipped to handle complex requirements.
